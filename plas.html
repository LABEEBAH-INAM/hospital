Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery Excellence

The Department of Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery at LNH is amongst the oldest Plastic Surgical units of Pakistan. It provides comprehensive reconstructive, cosmetic, burns and hand surgery services to patients. The department consists of well trained and experienced surgeons with extensive local and foreign experience.
