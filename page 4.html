Simulation Instructors’ Workshop



Introduction


This 3-day workshop is designed for health professionals and educators with the aim of incorporating simulation as a mode of instruction and quality improvement in healthcare. It serves as an introduction to the world of healthcare simulation and provides means of implementing sim in their own settings and with the resources that are available. We plan to expand this program into a Certification Program in Simulations.


Program Details


  • Duration: 3 days
  • Mode of Education: Online/Hands-on
  • Classes in a Week: 3 days workshop
  • Duration of Class: 8 hours
  • Timing: 9am – 5pm

Course Organizers


  • Department of Emergency Medicine, IHHN, Karachi
  • CME accredited by Hammad Medical Qatar

Teaching Methodologies


For Online


Zoom sessions, and Google Docs for live collaborative work. Virtual Resuscitation Room (VRR)


For In-person


  • Zoom for taking international faculty on board,
  • Hands-on Simulation: Simple App/Resus Monitor, Moulage materials, mannequins and task trainers (rented/DIY)
  • VRR and Full code for introduction to online/distant simulation.

Program Objectives


  • To introduce simulation and its role in healthcare.
  • To develop a pool of leaders and health profession educators with interest and knowledge to start implementing simulation as a method of quality improvement and education.
  • To develop champions of simulation who can then play their role in spreading this even further.

Scope of Practice


This workshop is an introduction to the world of healthcare simulation and is targeted at an audience that is interested in implementing simulation as a mode of education and quality improvement.
