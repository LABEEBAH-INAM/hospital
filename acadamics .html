ACADAMICS

Continuing Professional Education



DHIPC

The one-year institutional diploma program will prepare healthcare professionals to lead and implement comprehensive infection prevention and control strategies in integrated healthcare facilities.


Certification Prog in Emergency Medicine

The Certification Program in Emergency Medicine (CPEM) is a 12-month competency-based training that aims to equip physicians working in emergency departments (EDs) with fundamental knowledge and expertise in the care of patients who are severely ill or injured.


Foundation Prog in Pediatric Emergency Medicine

The program aims to equip physicians working in the Emergency Departments (EDs) with the fundamental knowledge and clinical expertise in the practice of pediatric emergency care.


Simulation Instructors’ Workshop

This 3-day workshop is designed for health professionals and educators with the aim of incorporating simulation as a mode of instruction and quality improvement in healthcare.
