Successful Treatment of Infra-Renal Aneurysm through Innovative Minimally Invasive Procedure

By Dr. Jeewan Pillai, Dr. Sameer Gupta, Dr. Prashanta Bolthakur in Metro Hospital and Heart Institute, Sector 12, Noida

In a remarkable display of expertise and innovation, the esteemed doctors at Metro Hospital, Noida, led by Dr. Sameer Gupta, Dr.Jeewan Pillai, and Dr. Prasanta Borthakur, recently conducted a complex surgical procedure to treat a patient suffering from an infra-renal aneurysm. This unique case presented significant challenges, but the skilled medical team’s collaborative efforts and pioneering approach resulted in a successful outcome.
