Department of E.N.T-Head and Neck Surgery :

The Department of Otorhinolaryngology and Head-and-Neck Surgery (ENT) has been providing diagnosis and comprehensive treatments of ear, nose and throat diseases as well as treatments for hearing and balance and head and neck cancer surgeries. The department has continuously evolved and flourished with the aim of providing quality care to patients and their families. Now, it is one of the major tertiary referral units in Pakistan. The department comprises of highly qualified consultants, excellent out-patient, in-patient, emergency and operative services along with diagnostic facilities for all ENT and head and neck diseases. Patients are managed in a systematic manner with emphasis on quick diagnosis and early treatment. There is a Procedure room in OPD for minor procedures, wound management and microscopic ear examination. Diagnostic fiberoptic / rigid endoscopic facilities are also available. Emergency cover is provided round the clock. We are looking forward to increase the skills and practice in world’s most advanced procedures, such as, Endoscopic skull base surgery, Endoscopic per oral thyroidectomy and Cochlear implant
