CENTRES

Our Centers

Diagnostic Center

Advanced diagnostics including MRI, CT scans, and lab tests.

Our Diagnostic Center provides comprehensive imaging services to ensure accurate diagnoses, facilitating timely and effective treatment for patients.

Cancer Center

Comprehensive cancer care with personalized treatment plans.

We offer state-of-the-art treatment options, including chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and support services to assist patients and families during their journey.

Heart Center

Specialized heart care and advanced cardiac procedures.

Our Heart Center focuses on diagnosing and treating cardiovascular conditions, providing a range of services from prevention to complex cardiac interventions.

Community Health Center

Preventive health services and community outreach programs.

The Community Health Center emphasizes preventive care and health education, aiming to improve overall community well-being and access to healthcare services.

CAMBRIDGE HOSPITAL

Cambridge Memorial Hospital is a vibrant full service, acute care hospital committed to providing exceptional health care to the communities of Cambridge, North Dumfries and the region of Waterloo with a mission to "Create Healthier Communities, Together."
