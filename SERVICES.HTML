SERVICES

MRI

Magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) provides detailed images of soft tissues to help diagnose various conditions such as tumors, brain disorders, and more.

X-ray

X-rays use ionizing radiation to create images of bones and organs, commonly used for diagnosing fractures and chest-related conditions.

CT Scan

CT scans produce cross-sectional images of the body using X-rays to provide a clearer view of internal structures for diagnosing complex conditions.

Ultrasound

Ultrasound imaging uses sound waves to capture real-time images of the body's organs and tissues, commonly used in pregnancy and soft tissue diagnosis.

Echo

An echocardiogram (Echo) provides detailed images of the heart, helping to assess heart function and detect conditions like valve issues or heart failure.

Laboratory

Our laboratory offers a range of diagnostic tests, including blood work, urine analysis, and microbiological tests, to aid in diagnosis and treatment plans.

DNA Testing

DNA testing provides genetic insights for diagnosing hereditary conditions and assists in personalized medical treatment plans.

First Aid

Our first aid services offer immediate care for injuries, illnesses, and emergencies, ensuring rapid stabilization of patients.

Dialysis

Dialysis services help patients with kidney failure by removing waste and excess fluid from the blood, ensuring optimal health management.

Pediatrics

Our pediatrics department provides specialized care for infants, children, and adolescents, focusing on growth, development, and health management.

Dental Care

We offer comprehensive dental care services, including routine check-ups, cleanings, fillings, and treatments for gum disease and other oral health issues.

Vaccinations

We provide a full range of vaccinations for children and adults, including routine immunizations and travel vaccinations to prevent diseases.

Blood Tests

Our laboratory offers blood tests to help diagnose various health conditions, including cholesterol levels, diabetes, and blood disorders.

Pharmacy

Our on-site pharmacy provides prescription medications, over-the-counter drugs, and expert advice on managing your health.

Emergency Services

We offer 24/7 emergency services with specialized medical teams ready to provide urgent care for critical injuries and illnesses.
